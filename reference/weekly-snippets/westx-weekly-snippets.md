Snippets - A Guide
This is what good looks like! This document can be shared widely.

Why Snippets? They are a digestible format to share information with one another and with leadership. They are a quick pulse on the business, weekly, to make sure we are quickly identifying opportunities and trends, addressing risks, and working on the most important issues. Snippets are also an opportunity to celebrate great work and the contributions of your peers across GitHub.
Snippet structure: 
- Should be 1-3 sentences, max
- Should be consumable by someone with limited context on the topic - write simply and clearly, in plain language, and re-read to ensure that it will be comprehensible to a wide audience. 
- Limit use of acronyms to those you would expect to be understood by nearly all Revenue Hubbers
- Do not use project names (i.e., Project Abba) without a brief description of the project for context
 - Please follow the structure below:
