## Customer Outcomes Vision Statement

Our vision is to **accelerate customer growth** through the GitHub platform, with **GitHub Copilot at the forefront** of driving measurable business outcomes.

**As product and technical experts**, we operate as a **unified** Customer Outcomes team, driving exceptional results through forecast and **ownership of adoption, retention, and expansion** (CSQL) opportunities, with a **relentless** focus on customer health and speed to value.

## Four Adoption Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **1. Vision & Value** | Align on objectives, sponsorship, and goals |
| **2. GitHub Platform** | Maintain a healthy, well-architected platform |
| **3. Skills & Capability Building** | Enablement, champions, and certifications |
| **4. Developer Experience** | Drive usage, productivity, and advocacy |

## Risk CTA Process

### Risk Types

| Category | Type | Description |
|----------|------|-------------|
| External | Layoffs / RIF | Dev population decreases |
| External | M&A | Company acquired by competitor-platform user |
| External | Macroeconomic | Business struggles from environment/policy |
| External | Technical | Outage, Incident/Bug, Platform Issues |
| Adoption | Failure to Launch | Initial activation failure or users fall off quickly |
| Adoption | Technical Blockers | Security reviews, provisioning, migrations |
| Adoption | IT Gatekeepers | POCs fail to move forward or resist change |
